English is one of the most important languages to learn, and the most important language to know in the United States. The official language in the United States is English, therefore, in order to communicate effectively, it is important that you gain proficiency in the language.

There are many different platforms to learn the language, such as through the internet, in person classes, and books/other text based resources.

Learning Online

Learning a language online gives you the flexibility and ease of access that a tutor or class might not provide.


The Pros and Cons of Learning a Language Online:

PROS:

    Ability to choose teachers from all over the world which helps students understand different types of pronunciation
    Flexible schedules with very few, or no, limits which allows the student to study as much as they want

CONS:
    Unable to get internet access/internet slowdowns
    No push to study/learn compared to a teacher in a class

Learning In-Person

Learning a language in person provides a student with a structured curriculum with instant feedback.



The Pros and Cons of Learning a Language through an In-Person Class:

PROS:
    Accountability - certain times and schedules
    Instant feedback from the teachers
    Typically a greater emphasis on grammar

CONS:
    Greater emphasis on a set curriculum
    Typically costs more
    Posibility of conflicting schedules

Learning through Books

Learning a language using textbook resources gives the student a strong foundation in vocabulary and grammar.



The Pros and Cons of Learning a Language through Paper Resources:

PROS:
    Access to grammar, vocabulary, and other resources
    More structured
    Typically a greater emphasis on grammar

CONS:
    No feedback
    Limited practice
    Outdated/aged
