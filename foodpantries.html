Food Resources

A great place to get food are at your local food banks and pantries. They are welcoming to all and provide the necessary resources to create your daily meals! Below are a few good food pantries and food banks in Austin Of course, there are many more to be discovered around the city! For more information on the below mentioned areas, make sure to check out there website.

Central Texas Food Bank
With the partnership of 21 agencies across different counties in Central Texas such as HEB, the Central Texas Food Bank has helped thousands of people who are in need of food.

Hope Food Pantry of Austin
The mission of the hope food pantry is to alleviate hunger around communities. Volunteers here have been supplying Austin families with a variety of groceries since 2000.

Manos de Cristo
The Manos de Cristo food pantry is always open from monday to friday from 8am to 11am, providing emergency assistance to individuals and families. Manos de Cristo receives donations from individuals, local ministries, and other organizations. Furthermore, they purchase any other needed resources.
